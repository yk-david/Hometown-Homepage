Visit the Ancient Roman Theaters

Ancient Roman architecture, outdoor performances, and the ‘Nuits de Fourvière’ festival.

Relax Aboard a Sightseeing Cruise

Guided commentary, gourmet meal, and scenic views of Lyon from the water.
